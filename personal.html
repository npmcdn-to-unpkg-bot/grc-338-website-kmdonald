PERSONAL

What I like to do

In my free time I love to adventure and spend time with my friends. I love going on hikes, enjoying the outdoors, and taking in the beauty of nature. That being said, I also love spending quality time on my couch relaxing.

I'm from Orange County, California and one of my favorite parts of going back home is getting to hang out with my dog. My hometown is a wonderful area and I can often be found spending time with friends and taking pictures of the beautiful scenes of nature.

I also love sports and am a huge Broncos and Angels fan. In high school I ran cross country and track, and played soccer, so I love breaking out the running shoes or soccer cleats now.

My go-to music is Country and I enjoy breaking out my cowboys boots and going line-dancing with my friends. One of my favorite experiences from last year was going to the Stagecoach Music Festival and seeing some of my favorite artists live.

Getting to live in San Luis Obispo is such a privilege and I plan on taking advantage of all the fun things to around here for the next year until I graduate!
